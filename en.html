Profile

From single-seaters to prototypes — a career built at full speed. At 22, Doriane Pin is the 2025 F1 Academy world champion and a full-time endurance prototype driver in 2026. She is also a development driver for the Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS F1 Team.

Born in Ivry-sur-Seine, she started out in karting before making her mark in endurance and single-seater racing. Doriane Pin has established herself as one of the most complete drivers of her generation: champion in Ferrari Challenge Europe, class winner at the 24 Hours of Spa, podium on her LMP2 debut, an F4 race win — versatility is her signature.

In 2026 she is a full-time LMP2 driver in the ELMS and at the 24 Hours of Le Mans with Duqueine, alongside development roles with Mercedes-AMG PETRONAS Formula 1 Team, Peugeot TotalEnergies for the Hypercar programme, and Citroën Racing in Formula E.
